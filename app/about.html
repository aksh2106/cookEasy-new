About us

Who we are and what we do?

“CookEasy” is a new generation online platform, dedicated to providing you with a simplified experience for your grocery shopping and making easy recipes out of them. With our tagline ‘Eat Amazing!’, CookEasy works with you towards fulfilling your wishes to eat your favorite delicacies sitting at your house by serving you from the kitchen to your plate. Our mascot, Cheffy, ‘The Owl’ will make cooking a breeze.

For quite some time now, people have transitioned from a traditional grocery shopping to an online experience. Yet, no one got any closer to making your cooking experience easier than us. This is an untapped niche segment where CookEasy wants to make its mark by targeting an audience who wish to cook their favorite food in a healthy way at home. CookEasy furnishes for them a joint experience of online grocery shopping and further assisting them in making their wish for a home cooked meal possible. Our target audience includes working professionals, homemakers, individuals learning to cook, students, and a good proportion of users interested in food art.


CookEasy is founded with the idea of bridging the gap between your mood to taste delicacies at the comfort of your home and the tiring grocery shopping. CookEasy intelligently lets you order the ingredients needed for a recipe on your mind and delivers a fresh bunch of ingredients right at your doorstep. Taking a step forward, our mascot Cheffy ‘The Owl’ provides you a clear set of instructions to prepare your recipe once the items have been delivered.
